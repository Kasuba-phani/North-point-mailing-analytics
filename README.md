# North-point-mailing-analytics
Predictive modeling and customer segmentation analysis to optimize direct mailing campaigns for North-Point Software. Includes Classification, Regression, and Clustering models.

Project Goal: Increase profitability of direct mailing campaigns by selecting the top 200,000 prospects from a pool of 5 million.

Methodology:

Classification: Used Decision Tree, Logistic Regression, and Random Forest to predict purchase probability.

Regression: Used Linear, Random Forest, and Stepwise Regression to predict expected spending.

Clustering: Used K-Means to segment customers into "VIP", "Warm", and "Cold" tiers.

Key Results:

Best Model: Random Forest achieved 81.7% accuracy in predicting purchasers.

Business Impact: Identifying the top decile of customers yields a 1.9x Lift over random selection.

Drivers: Transaction frequency and recency of updates were the strongest predictors of spending.
